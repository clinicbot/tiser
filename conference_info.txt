SOCIAL PROGRAM & TOURS
==============================================================================
COCKTAIL EVENT
- When: Thursday, June 4, 2026, 18:00
- Where: at the Museum of Tolerance, after the scientific program
- A cocktail menu with evening refreshments and a beverage bar, accompanied by
  a live musical ensemble. Included in registration.

EVENING TOURS — Thursday, June 4, 2026
- All tours require a minimum of 25 participants.

Tour A — Machane Yehuda Night Market Tour
- Departure: 19:00 from the Museum of Tolerance plaza (return transport included)
- Duration: approximately 2 hours
- Cost: 250 NIS per person
- A nighttime walk through Jerusalem's Machane Yehuda market, sampling local
  cocktails (including homemade limoncello and strawberry liqueur) and visiting
  bars in the Nachlaot neighborhood, including the first Balkan bar in Israel.

Tour B — National Library of Israel Tour
- Departure: 19:00 from the Museum of Tolerance plaza (return transport included)
- Duration: approximately 1.5 hours
- Cost: 45 NIS per person
- A visit featuring rare books, ancient manuscripts, photo albums, unique maps,
  historical posters, and rotating exhibitions including artist installations.

Tour C — "06:29 — From Darkness to Light" Exhibition Tour
- Meeting point: 19:30 at the museum entrance
- Duration: approximately 45 minutes
- Cost: 45 NIS per person
- An exhibition documenting the events of October 7 through personal testimonies
  and documentary filmmaking.

ABSTRACTS & RESEARCH PRIZE
==============================================================================
- Abstract / research-prize submission deadline was March 25, 2026 (now closed).
- Abstracts: English only; structured as Background, Methods, Results,
  Conclusions; maximum 300 words (excluding title, authors, affiliations).
- Accepted abstracts appear in the digital abstract book and the program as
  submitted. Acceptance and presentation format (oral or e-poster) were sent by
  the end of April 2026.
- Research Prize: open to residents/trainees only. Submitted separately from the
  abstract (to shaiel@kldltd.com, subject line noting "research prize
  submission"); maximum 5 pages; Arial 11, 1.5 line spacing, English.
